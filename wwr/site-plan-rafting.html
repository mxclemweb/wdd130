Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide people with a fun experience on rivers with family and friends. We hope to reach a larger audience to inspire people to come experience the fun of white water rafting.

Audience

Target customers include families, young adults, and anyone who enjoys the outdoors. These people want fun experiences they can have and share with friends and family. They also want recreation something that is enjoyable and remedies the monotony and stress of daily struggles. This site will gear towards motivating the audience to use our services. This site will also answer questions about safety, rafting guides, scheduling, what they would bring or wear, prices, locations, about white water rafting and more. The needs that would be met are adventure, fun, shared experiences, vacation, outdoor recreation, thrill, etc. Audiences would acces through browsers on any device from desktops, laptops, phones, tablets, etc.
